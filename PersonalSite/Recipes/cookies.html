Chocolate Chip Cookies

< Back To Desserts

Ingredients

  • 1 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup butter (softened)
  • 1/3 cup brown sugar
  • 1/3 cup white sugar
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 egg
  • Chocolate chips (measured with the heart ðŸ’–)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350Â°F (175Â°C).
  2. In a bowl, mix softened butter, brown sugar, and white sugar until smooth.
  3. Add egg and vanilla extract, mixing until combined.
  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together flour, baking soda, and salt.
  5. Gradually fold dry ingredients into the wet mixture.
  6. Stir in chocolate chips.
  7. Scoop dough onto a baking sheet
  8. Bake for 9 minutes or until edges are lightly golden.
  9. Let cookies cool on the tray for 2 minutes before transferring to a wire rack.
