Mac & Cheese

Back to Dinners | Home

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 cups whole milk
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 pinches ground nutmeg
  • 2 pinches ground pepper
  • 2 cups shredded cheese (cheddar or mix)
  • 2 tbsp grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 pound macaroni noodles
  • Breadcrumbs (optional, for topping)

Instructions

  1. Boil macaroni noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a saucepan, melt butter over medium heat. Add flour and whisk to create a roux.
  3. Slowly add milk while whisking, and let it thicken.
  4. Stir in salt, nutmeg, and pepper.
  5. Add shredded cheese and Parmesan, stirring until melted.
  6. Mix in cooked noodles and stir well.
  7. (Optional) Transfer to a baking dish, sprinkle breadcrumbs on top, and broil until golden brown.
  8. Serve hot and enjoy!
