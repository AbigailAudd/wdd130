Egg Gravy

Back to Dinners | Home

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 25 fl oz milk
  • 10 Hard boiled eggs

Instructions

  1. Melt butter in a sauce pan
  2. Whisk in flour
  3. Slowly add milk while whisking, and let it thicken.
  4. Add mashed hard boiled eggs
  5. Serve over biscuits or toast!
