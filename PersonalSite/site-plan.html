Dinners

Mac & Cheese
ingredients: 2 tbsp butter, 2 tbsp all purpose flour, 1 1/2 cups whole milk, 1/2 tsp salt, 2 big pinches ground nutmeg, 2 big pinches gound pepper, 2 cups shredded cheese, 2 tbsp grated parmesan cheese, 1/2 pound macaroni noodles.
Recipe: Boil noodles, create rous and add seasonings and cheese, put cooked noodles in and stir, add breadcurmbs on top and broil until golden brown.

Desserts

Cookies
ingredients: 1 1/4 cup flour, 1/2 tsp baking soda, 1/2 tsp salt, 1/2 cup butter, 1/3 cup brown sugar, 1/3 cup white sugar, 1/2 tsp vanilla extract, 1 egg, chocolate chips (measured with the heart)
Preheat oven to 350, mix wet ingredients, fold in dry ingredients and chocolate chips, scoop into balls and bake for 9 minutes, let cool on tray for 2 minutes and then continue to cool on cooling rack.
