Brazilian Beans and Rice

< Back To Dinners

Ingredients

  • 2 cans black beans
  • 1/2 lb andouille sausage sliced
  • 1 Tbsp Olive oil
  • 1/2 Onion diced
  • 3 Cloves garlic
  • 2 Bay leaves
  • 1/2 tsp Oregeno
  • 1 Cup Chicken broth
  • White Rice

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a large pot and saute onions, garlic and sausage
  2. once onions are translucent, add in beans, bay leaves, oregano, and chicken broth
  3. Simmer for 20 minutes
  4. Remove bay leaves and serve over rice
